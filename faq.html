Frequently Asked Questions

Can solar power handle the heavy energy requirements of my factory?

Yes. Solar solutions can be designed to reliably meet even high industrial energy demands.

Will installing solar reduce my household electricity bills?

Yes. Solar energy significantly lowers monthly electricity expenses.

Can my home stay powered during EB outages with solar?

Yes. With the right setup, your home can enjoy uninterrupted power during EB cuts.

How much electricity can a solar installation produce?

Output depends on system size and sunlight, but installations are designed to meet daily needs.

What is the expected lifespan of a solar installation?

Quality installations typically last 25 years or more with proper maintenance.

What warranties and guarantees does RI Solar provide?

RI Solar offers warranties on panels, inverters, and installation, along with a 72-hour replacement policy for urgent issues.

What components are included in a rooftop solar installation?

A system generally includes solar panels, mounting structures, inverters, wiring, junction boxes, protection devices, and batteries (if required).

Will solar continue to work during rainy or cloudy weather?

Yes. Systems generate electricity even on cloudy days, though output may be lower.

Can RI Solar customize a solution for my home or business?

Yes. Every installation is tailored to your space, energy requirements, and goals.

How much space is needed for a solar installation?

A complete 1 kW solar power plant needs about 70 sq.ft. of open, shadow-free space. The exact area scales with plant capacity. Use our Solar Calculator for an estimate.

Is maintaining an RI Solar installation expensive?

No. These systems are low-maintenance and cost-effective.

What is the recommended way to clean solar panels?

Regular cleaning with a tap water sprinkler or soft cloth helps maintain efficiency.

Can I switch off my solar installation when not in use?

Yes. You can operate it according to your needs.

Can a solar installation be upgraded if my energy needs grow?

Yes. Systems can be scaled up or expanded as required.

Is solar suitable for apartments or rented properties?

Yes. Flexible solutions are available for various property types.

How many units of electricity does a 1 kW solar installation generate?

On average, a 1 kW system generates 4–5 units per day, depending on sunlight and location.

Are government subsidies available for solar installations?

Yes. Under the PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana, homeowners can receive subsidies of up to ₹78,000, depending on system size.
